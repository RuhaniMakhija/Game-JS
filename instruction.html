Instructions to play Dragon Leap!

  • The game will begin with the dragon positioned at the starting point.
  • As the game progresses, obstacles will appear on the screen, moving towards the dragon.
  • Your goal is to help the dragon leap over these obstacles.
  • You can move the dragon by left, right and Up key of keyboard.
  • Each successful jump over an obstacle will earn you points, increasing your score. Aim to accumulate as many points as possible.
  • Be careful not to let the dragon collide with any obstacles. If the dragon hits an obstacle, the game will end.
  • As you continue playing, the speed of the obstacles will gradually increase, making the game more challenging.
  • Remember, practice makes perfect! With time and experience, you'll improve your reflexes and timing.
